We are a proud-standing airline, that has vowed to always offer the best comfort and service on-board our planes.
Chao Air™ was founded during the summer season of 2025 with the help of our parent company Sonic™ Team, and from there we started our jurney releasing the first Chao Air™ airliner; The A320-200 "Neutral Chao" (EI-CHA), sadly not operative anymore.
We then chose that the standard for our fleet was to be registred in Ireland for reducing costs.
Thereafter, we began flying European routes, for example from Milan Malpensa (LIMC) to Copenhagen Kastrup (EKCH) with an encreasing amount of aircrafts with their own passion-crafted liveries. Various of them depicting Sonic The Hedgehog™ characters.

Once our fleet was initialized, we became quite popular in Italy and Switzerland, bringing up the need to created country-based liveries, for example an Italian one - this idea is still a working progress, so things can change at any moment.
We are working on a lot of additions for the airline, and we have just started our enterprise's journey.
In conclusion, we wouldn't be here without the help from donations and fans, we are very thankfull for all of you!
